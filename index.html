About the catalog

RVMT was assembled from 5,150 globally distributed metatranscriptomes, expanding the known RNA-virosphere across all five established phyla and adding several proposed new ones.

Phyla

Six classified phyla plus candidate clades — Lenarviricota, Pisuviricota, Kitrinoviricota, Duplornaviricota, Negarnaviricota, Ribozyviria.

Hosts

RNA bacteriophages dominate microbial communities; eukaryotic viruses span Metazoa, Viridiplantae, Fungi, and microbial eukaryotes.

Provenance

Aggregated from JGI's IMG/M, Callanan et al. 2020, Wolf et al. 2020, Lauber et al. 2019, the MATOU expedition, and RefSeq/GenBank.
